About CHILLOOZY

Treat yourself to a scoop of happiness today! Our ice cream is the ultimate summer treat, loved for its refreshing taste and creamy texture. At CHILLOOZY, we offer a variety of fruit-flavored ice creams that are not only delicious but packed with natural goodness. It’s the perfect combination of flavor and coolness! Whether you’re craving something sweet or a guilt-free dessert, our fruit ice creams are sure to delight your taste buds and cool you down on a hot day.

Special Menu

  • MANGOBERRY: A refreshing blend of mango and strawberry ice cream, crowned with toasted walnuts for a perfect crunch in every bite.
  • AVOTACHIOS: Creamy avocado ice cream, topped with finely ground pistachios for a rich, nutty flavor.
  • BANANAZOAT: A delightful combination of banana and strawberry-flavored ice cream, topped with crunchy oats and drizzled with rich chocolate syrup.
  • CINNAPLE: A fusion of cinnamon and lemon ice cream, topped with dried apples for a burst of flavor.
  • WATERMELONI: A blend of kiwi and watermelon ice cream, enhanced with chia seeds and smooth milk chocolate drizzle.
  • PEACHYNUTS: A combination of kiwi and peach ice cream, topped with Spanish peanuts and drizzled with caramel.
